45+ YEARS OF

ENGINEERING EXCELLENCE

Here at Hy-Ram Engineering we offer complete solutions for joining, installing and maintaining HDPE pipework through Electrofusion and Butt Fusion equipment for the water and gas industries.

Founded and based in the UK with over 45 years of engineering experience and knowledge, Hy-Ram Engineering now have a global presence and an international reputation for high quality and innovative products for the joining of plastic and metallic pipework.
